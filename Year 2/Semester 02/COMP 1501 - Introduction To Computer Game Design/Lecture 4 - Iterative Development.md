
#### Design through Iteration  
Rapid Prototyping and the Playcentric Approach
You design → you test with players → you learn → you redesign.

In all of our game making, we will be following the “Playcentric” model of design, or Iterative model.  

This model is about recognizing one fact:  
	We can’t know if a game is fun without playing it.


1. **Set the Experience Goal**  
	a. What experience do you want the player to have?  
2. **Prototype and Play Early**  
	a. The second you have an idea, make it playable  
	b. It can be ugly, on paper, using cue cards and whiteboards, but it has rules and can be played  
	c. Critique your game: What the worst, most boring part?  
3. **Iterate**  
	a. Find the biggest flaw, fix it, play it, try again.  
	b. What’s the best part? Enhance it, play it, try again.
