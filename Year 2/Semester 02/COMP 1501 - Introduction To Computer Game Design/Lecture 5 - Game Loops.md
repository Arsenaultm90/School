#### Mental Model
What does the player know?  
What does the player expect?  
What questions, goals do they have?  
What’s motivating them to play?

#### Take Action
Do they know which actions are available?  
Which actions can they take?  
Which actions do they want to take?  
Can they perform the action?

#### Rules
The game reacts, data changes.

#### Feedback
How do you tell the player how the world changed?  
How do you tell the player they did good/bad?  
... moved closer/farther from their goal?  
Rewards, visual effects, sounds, story.


#### Arcs
Arcs are basically just loops that don’t loop and emphasize feedback. Think movies.
