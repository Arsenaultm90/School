
**Quality Assurance:** 
	Playing a part of the game repeatedly to certify the game for a console or identify breaking issues with the game that require technical fixes.  

**Internal Review:** 
	The team making the game plays it to discuss features.  

**Playtesting:** 
	Having players play for fun in order to identify potential issues with your design; helps answer questions about “fun”.


#### Common QA Questions
1. What was the most frustrating moment or aspect of what you just played?  
2. What was your favourite moment or aspect of what you just played?  
3. Was there anything you wanted to do that you couldn’t?  
4. If you had a magic wand to wave, and you could change, add, or remove anything from the experience, what would it be?  
5. What were you doing in the experience?  
6. How would you describe this game to your friends and family?
